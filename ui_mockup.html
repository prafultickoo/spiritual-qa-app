What is the meaning of dharma?
10:30 AM
Dharma is your righteous path in life - your duty, purpose, and the natural order that guides your actions. It encompasses both universal principles of righteousness and your personal life mission.

Think of dharma as the river that flows naturally toward the ocean. When you follow your dharma, life flows with greater ease and meaning.

How do I find my dharma?
10:32 AM
To discover your dharma, observe what brings you joy and serves others simultaneously. Your dharma lies at the intersection of your natural talents, what the world needs, and what brings you deep fulfillment.

Practice meditation, self-reflection, and pay attention to what activities make you lose track of time.
